Currency details

NNPP is an ERC20 (BEP20) token. It lives in the Binance Smart Chain.

The token's features are:

  • • Prizes in coins to all active users
  • • 40% of the total supply burnt
  • • 40% of the total supply in PancakeSwap
  • • The first NNPP transactions are with 0% fees, be the first to buy it!
  • • NNPP subsequent transactions carry a small 2% fee in NNPP
  • • Marketing account to advertise $NNPP all over the globe
  • • Small gas fee thanks to being on Binance Smart Chain


The NNPP fee is distributed to the marketing account (0.1%) and to the pool (1.9%).

For any transaction the sender may win a part of the pool prize. Up to 40% of the whole pool prize can be won by the sender of any transaction.

To get your chance of winning the prizes, make a transaction in NNPP. Winners are randomly chosen ON EACH TRANSACTION!
